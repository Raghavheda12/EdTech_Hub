Google LLC is a global technology company headquartered in Mountain View, California. Founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin in 1998, Google is best known for its search engine, which dominates the global market. The company has expanded its portfolio to include Android, Google Chrome, Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Cloud. Google also develops hardware like the Pixel smartphones, Nest smart home devices, and Chromebook laptops. With a strong focus on artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and digital advertising, Google continues to be a leader in the tech industry, shaping the future of the digital world.
