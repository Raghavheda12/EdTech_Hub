Apple Inc. is a global technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976, Apple is best known for its iconic products like the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, and Apple Watch. The company has expanded its portfolio to include services such as iCloud, Apple Music, Apple TV+, and the App Store. Apple also develops hardware like the AirPods, HomePod, and various accessories. With a strong focus on innovation, design, and user experience, Apple continues to be a leader in the tech industry, shaping the future of personal computing and digital entertainment.
