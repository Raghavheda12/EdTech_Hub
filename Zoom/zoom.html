Zoom Video Communications, Inc. is a global technology company headquartered in San Jose, California. Founded by Eric Yuan in 2011, Zoom is best known for its video conferencing platform, which has revolutionized virtual communication worldwide. The company offers a suite of products, including Zoom Meetings, Zoom Webinars, Zoom Phone, and Zoom Rooms, catering to businesses, educators, and individuals. Zoom also provides enterprise solutions for collaboration, cloud telephony, and hybrid work environments. With a strong focus on innovation, security, and user experience, Zoom continues to be a leader in video communication, shaping the future of remote work and digital connectivity.
