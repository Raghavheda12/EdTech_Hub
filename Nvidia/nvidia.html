NVIDIA Corporation is a global technology company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. Founded by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem in 1993, NVIDIA is best known for its graphics processing units (GPUs), which dominate the gaming and professional markets. The company has expanded its portfolio to include AI solutions, deep learning, and high-performance computing technologies. NVIDIA also develops software frameworks like CUDA, as well as hardware such as the Tesla and Quadro GPUs, and the DRIVE platform for autonomous vehicles. With a strong focus on artificial intelligence, data centers, and gaming, NVIDIA continues to be a leader in the tech industry, driving advancements in graphics, AI, and computing power.
