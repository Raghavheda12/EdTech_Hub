Stripe, Inc. is a global financial technology company headquartered in San Francisco, California. Founded by Patrick and John Collison in 2010, Stripe provides a suite of payment processing solutions that power millions of businesses worldwide. The company enables secure online transactions, offering services such as Stripe Payments, Stripe Connect, and Stripe Atlas. Stripe also supports businesses with financial infrastructure, including billing, fraud prevention, and revenue management tools. With a strong focus on innovation, developer-friendly APIs, and global scalability, Stripe continues to shape the future of digital commerce and financial technology.

Products & Services

Payments Processing

Stripe Payments: A powerful payment processing platform that enables businesses to accept online payments securely and efficiently.

Stripe Terminal: A solution for in-person payments, integrating online and offline payment experiences seamlessly.

Financial Infrastructure

Stripe Connect: A customizable platform that allows businesses to manage payments, onboard users, and handle global payouts.

Stripe Atlas: A tool that helps entrepreneurs and startups easily incorporate their businesses in the U.S.

Learn More

Billing & Subscriptions

Stripe Billing: A comprehensive solution for managing recurring payments, invoices, and subscriptions.

Stripe Invoicing: A fast and efficient invoicing system that allows businesses to send and collect payments globally.

Learn More

Fraud Prevention & Security

Stripe Radar: An advanced fraud detection and prevention system powered by machine learning.

3D Secure: A secure authentication protocol that helps protect businesses and customers from fraudulent transactions.

Learn More

Developer & API Tools

Stripe API: A developer-friendly API that allows seamless integration of payments and financial services into websites and apps.

Stripe CLI: A command-line tool for testing and managing Stripe integrations more efficiently.
